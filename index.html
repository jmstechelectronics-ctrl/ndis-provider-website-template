FAQ

Helpful answers that reduce hesitation before you contact us.

These are the questions people usually ask before they pick up the phone or send an enquiry.

Who do you support?

Harbour Lane Support works with adults and families who need practical NDIS support, especially around Support Coordination, Daily Living Supports, Community Access and Plan Management.

What funding types do you accept?

We currently work with self-managed, plan-managed and NDIA-managed participants. If you are unsure what applies, we can talk it through with you.

What happens after I contact you?

We ask a few simple questions about the person, location and support needed, then let you know whether we are the right fit and what the next step looks like.

How quickly do you respond?

Most calls and enquiries are answered within one business day. If we need more detail before confirming fit, we will let you know clearly.

Do you service my suburb?

We support participants across the Northern Rivers including Ballina, Lismore, Byron Bay and nearby communities. If you are just outside those areas, ask and we will confirm availability.

How do feedback and complaints work?

You can raise feedback or concerns by phone, email or through our complaints page. We take concerns seriously, explain the process clearly and document follow-up steps.

Prefer a callback instead?

If a quick chat is easier, request a callback and we can talk through service area, support type and next steps without pressure.
