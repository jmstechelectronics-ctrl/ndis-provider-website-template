Feedback and Complaints

Harbour Lane Support welcomes feedback and takes complaints seriously. This page outlines how concerns can be raised, acknowledged and followed up in a respectful and timely way.

How to give feedback

Participants, families and referrers can usually share feedback by phone, email or in writing. Feedback should be welcomed and acknowledged respectfully.

How complaints are handled

Complaints should be recorded, acknowledged promptly, investigated fairly and resolved with clear follow-up communication.

If more support is needed

Where appropriate, add details for external complaint options such as the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission.
