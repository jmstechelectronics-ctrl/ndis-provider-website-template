Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Harbour Lane Support collects, uses, stores and protects personal information when participants, families, referrers and other visitors contact the service.

Information we collect

Typical enquiry details may include name, phone number, email address, suburb or region and information about support needs.

How information is used

Information is usually used to respond to enquiries, confirm service fit, manage referrals and provide ongoing support where appropriate.

Access and updates

Participants, families and referrers should have a clear way to request access to or correction of their personal information.

Questions

For privacy questions, contact hello@harbourlanesupport.com.au or call 0400 555 123.
