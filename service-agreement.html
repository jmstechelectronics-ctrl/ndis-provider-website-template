Service Agreement

This service agreement outlines the usual expectations between Harbour Lane Support and the participant, including how support is delivered, how scheduling works and how changes are communicated.

What the agreement covers

A service agreement usually explains the supports being delivered, how sessions are scheduled, how changes are handled and what each party can expect.

Fees and cancellations

List pricing, invoicing terms and any cancellation notice periods using wording that matches the provider’s real process.

Questions before signing

The provider should encourage participants, nominees and families to ask questions before anything starts.
